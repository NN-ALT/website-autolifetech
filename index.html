Dementia – Loved and Not Forgotten

Grandma’s dementia was worsening. She often forgot to take her medication and occasionally wandered out of the house in the middle of the night, leaving the family in constant worry.
We installed a smart door lock and CCTV system to monitor when Grandma entered or exited the home. The family now enjoys peace of mind. They receive alerts if Grandma leaves at odd hours, and they can ensure her medication routine is maintained—keeping her safe and cared for, always.
